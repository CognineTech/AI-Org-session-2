How a Baby Learns

From repeated exposure to shapes, sounds, and context, recognition improves over time — similar to deep models trained on large datasets.

What “Deep” Means

The term deep refers to multiple layers between input and output. More layers let the system learn increasingly complex and abstract patterns from raw data.

Why this changed everything

Instead of hand-coding every feature, networks learn what matters directly from data. This shift unlocks vision, language, and speech breakthroughs.
